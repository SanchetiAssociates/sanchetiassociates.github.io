About the Firm

Who We Are

S. S. Sancheti & Associates is a Chartered Accountancy firm based in Mumbai, providing end-to-end tax, audit, regulatory, advisory and CFO services to businesses, professionals, startups, corporates and family-managed enterprises.

Established in 2013, the firm is built on the philosophy of practical advice, strong technical depth, and client-centric execution. We work with clients as professional advisors and long-term business enablers.

Our approach blends domain knowledge, regulatory expertise, and commercial understanding, helping clients navigate evolving tax laws, financial regulations and business challenges with clarity and confidence.

🧠Strong technical foundation with a business-first approach
🎯Personalised solutions, not template-based advice
🔍Proactive risk identification at every engagement stage
🤝Long-term professional partnership with promoters and management
